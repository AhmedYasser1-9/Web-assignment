Chocolate Lava Cake

A decadent, rich chocolate dessert with a gooey, molten center.

Ingredients

1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup butter
1 cup powdered sugar
2 large eggs
2 egg yolks
6 tbsp all-purpose flour
Vanilla ice cream for serving

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Butter and flour four ramekins.
2
Microwave the chocolate and butter in a bowl until melted and smooth.
3
Stir in the powdered sugar, then whisk in the eggs and egg yolks.
4
Gently fold in the flour until just combined.
5
Divide the batter evenly among the ramekins and bake for 13-14 minutes.
6
Let sit for 1 minute, invert onto plates, and serve immediately with vanilla ice cream.
