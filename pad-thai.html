Vegetarian Pad Thai

A sweet and savory Thai street food classic made with rice noodles and tofu.

Ingredients

8 oz flat rice noodles
1 block firm tofu, cubed
2 cups bean sprouts
3 green onions, chopped
1/4 cup crushed peanuts
3 tbsp Pad Thai sauce
2 tbsp vegetable oil
1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions

1
Soak the rice noodles in warm water until soft, then drain.
2
Heat oil in a wok or large pan and pan-fry the tofu until golden.
3
Add the softened noodles and Pad Thai sauce, tossing constantly.
4
Stir in the bean sprouts and green onions, cooking for another 2 minutes.
5
Serve hot, topped with crushed peanuts and a squeeze of fresh lime.
