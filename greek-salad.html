Fresh Greek Salad

A crisp, refreshing salad bursting with Mediterranean flavors.

Ingredients

3 large tomatoes, chopped
1 cucumber, sliced
1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
1/2 cup Kalamata olives
1 block feta cheese
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
1 tsp dried oregano

Instructions

1
Combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and olives in a large serving bowl.
2
In a small jar, shake together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, and oregano.
3
Drizzle the dressing over the vegetables and toss gently.
4
Top with a whole block of feta cheese (or crumble it over the top).
5
Serve immediately at room temperature.
