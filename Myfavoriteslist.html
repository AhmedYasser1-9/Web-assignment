My Favorites List

    Breakfast & Brunch

  • Breakfast Tacos Bar: Set up a spread of warm corn or flour tortillas alongside bowls of fluffy scrambled eggs, sautéed peppers and onions, black beans, and roasted sweet potatoes. Offer toppings like shredded cheese, salsa, avocado, fresh cilantro, and hot sauce so everyone can customize their own perfect breakfast taco.

  • Savory Breakfast Bread Pudding: Tear day-old croissants or crusty bread into pieces and toss with sautéed mushrooms, caramelized onions, and Gruyère cheese in a buttered dish. Pour over a savory egg custard infused with thyme and let it soak before baking until golden and irresistibly puffed.

    • Appetizers & Snacks

  • Caprese Skewers: Thread a cherry tomato, a fresh basil leaf, and a mozzarella ball onto small skewers for a perfect, no-mess bite of a classic Italian salad . Arrange them on a platter and drizzle with balsamic glaze just before serving for effortless elegance .

  • Watermelon & Feta Bites: Top a cube of sweet, juicy watermelon with a cube of salty feta and a small mint leaf, securing it with a toothpick . Give them a final sprinkle of Tajín or chili-lime salt for a surprising and refreshing flavor explosion.

  • Pepper Jelly Cream Cheese: Place a block of softened cream cheese on a plate and smother it with spicy pepper jelly for a sweet, tangy, and creamy no-cook classic . Serve with sturdy crackers for a crowd-pleaser that disappears in minutes.

    • Desserts & Baking

  • Vanilla Sheet Cake with Buttercream: Bake a simple vanilla sheet cake in a half-sheet pan, then smother it with a thick layer of classic American buttercream for the ultimate nostalgic party dessert. Decorate with colorful sprinkles to match your theme, and you have a perfectly sliceable, crowd-feeding treat.

  • Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies: Cream together browned butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy, then beat in eggs and vanilla before folding in flour and plenty of high-quality chocolate chips. Scoop the dough onto baking sheets and bake until the edges are golden and the centers are perfectly soft and chewy.

  • Silky Chocolate Cream Pie: Bake a simple pie crust or use a store-bought Oreo crust, then fill it with a rich, silky stovetop pudding made from egg yolks, sugar, milk, and melted dark chocolate. Chill the pie until completely set, then top generously with lightly sweetened whipped cream and chocolate shavings.

    • Beverages

  • Classic Strawberry Banana Smoothie: Blend ripe bananas, frozen strawberries, creamy yogurt, and a splash of milk or orange juice until perfectly smooth and thick. This timeless combination is naturally sweet, crowd-pleasing, and comes together in minutes for an effortless treat.

  • Watermelon Mint Cooler: Feed chunks of seedless watermelon and a generous handful of fresh mint leaves through your juicer for an incredibly hydrating and refreshing drink. Serve immediately over ice with a lime wedge for the ultimate summer cooler that disappears fast.

  • Classic Mojito: Muddle fresh mint leaves with simple syrup and lime wedges in a sturdy glass to release their fragrant oils, then fill the glass with ice and a generous pour of white rum. Top it off with a splash of club soda, give it a quick stir, and garnish with a fresh mint sprig for the ultimate refreshing cocktail.

  • Classic Hot Chocolate: Warm whole milk in a saucepan and whisk in high-quality chopped dark chocolate until it's completely smooth and velvety, then stir in a pinch of salt and a drop of vanilla. Serve in mugs topped with a mountain of lightly sweetened whipped cream or a handful of mini marshmallows for pure comfort.
