Rustic Beef Stew

A slow-cooked, comforting stew packed with tender beef and root vegetables.

Ingredients

2 lbs beef chuck, cubed
3 carrots, chopped
3 potatoes, cubed
1 onion, chopped
4 cups beef broth
2 tbsp tomato paste
1 tsp dried thyme
2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

1
Heat olive oil in a large pot and brown the beef cubes on all sides.
2
Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
3
Stir in the tomato paste, thyme, and beef broth, scraping up any browned bits.
4
Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 1.5 hours.
5
Add the carrots and potatoes, and simmer for another 30 minutes until veggies are tender.
6
Season with salt and pepper and serve warm.
