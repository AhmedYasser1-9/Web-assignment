Spicy Chana Masala

A hearty and flavorful vegan Indian chickpea curry.

Ingredients

2 cans (15oz) chickpeas, rinsed
1 large onion, finely chopped
2 tomatoes, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp ginger, grated
2 tbsp garam masala
1 tsp turmeric
1 cup water
Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

1
Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in a pot until the onions are translucent.
2
Stir in the diced tomatoes, garam masala, and turmeric, cooking until tomatoes break down.
3
Add the chickpeas and water, bringing the mixture to a boil.
4
Reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens.
5
Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan bread.
