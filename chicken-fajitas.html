Sizzling Chicken Fajitas

Tender strips of seasoned chicken cooked with bell peppers and onions.

Ingredients

1 lb chicken breast, sliced
2 bell peppers, sliced
1 large onion, sliced
2 tbsp fajita seasoning
2 tbsp olive oil
8 flour tortillas
Sour cream and salsa for serving

Instructions

1
Toss the chicken slices with 1 tbsp of olive oil and the fajita seasoning.
2
Heat the remaining oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3
Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, then remove from pan.
4
In the same pan, sauté the onions and bell peppers until tender-crisp.
5
Return the chicken to the pan and toss everything together.
6
Serve warm with tortillas, sour cream, and salsa.
