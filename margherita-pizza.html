Classic Margherita Pizza

A traditional Neapolitan pizza with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil.

Ingredients

1 pizza dough
1/2 cup tomato sauce
8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
Fresh basil leaves
1 tbsp olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
2
Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
3
Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
4
Top with sliced mozzarella cheese.
5
Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
6
Garnish with fresh basil, a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper before serving.
