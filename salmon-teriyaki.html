Baked Salmon Teriyaki

Flaky salmon fillets glazed in a sweet and sticky homemade teriyaki sauce.

Ingredients

4 salmon fillets
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 tbsp honey
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tsp sesame oil
1 clove garlic, minced
Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2
In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and garlic.
3
Place salmon on the baking sheet and brush generously with the teriyaki glaze.
4
Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
5
Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions before serving.
