Gourmet Avocado Toast

A quick, healthy, and trendy breakfast topped with a perfectly poached egg.

Ingredients

2 slices thick sourdough bread
1 ripe avocado
2 large eggs
1 tsp lemon juice
Red pepper flakes
Sea salt and black pepper
1 tbsp white vinegar (for poaching)

Instructions

1
Toast the sourdough bread slices until golden and crispy.
2
Mash the avocado in a bowl with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
3
Bring a small pot of water to a simmer, add white vinegar, and create a gentle whirlpool.
4
Crack an egg into the center and poach for 3 minutes. Repeat with the second egg.
5
Spread the mashed avocado over the toast, top with a poached egg, and sprinkle with red pepper flakes.
